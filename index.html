About Company

A PARTNERSHIP
BUILT ON TRUST

KATCO is a joint venture between Orano (France) and Kazatomprom (Kazakhstan) — two of the world's foremost names in uranium production, united since 1996.

Operating in the Turkestan region, KATCO extracts uranium exclusively via the environmentally superior In-Situ Recovery (ISR) method, avoiding traditional open-pit mining and dramatically reducing surface disruption.

Our Technology

IN-SITU
RECOVERY
ISR METHOD

Unlike conventional open-pit or underground mining, ISR dissolves uranium directly in the ore body using a carefully controlled leaching solution — then pumps it to the surface for processing. No blasting. No excavation. No mountain of tailings.

The result: dramatically reduced surface disruption, lower carbon emissions, smaller water footprint, and safer working conditions — making ISR the environmental gold standard in uranium mining.

✓ No open-pit excavation
✓ 90% less surface disturbance
✓ Lower carbon footprint
